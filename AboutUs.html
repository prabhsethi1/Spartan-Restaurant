Founded in August 2021 by 4 local students

Spartan Restaurant caters to everyone who loves burgers, fries, and American food! We began our journey into opening this restaurant in 2021. Because of COVID-19, a lot of small restaurants were forced to close down, and we looked at it as an opportunity to open an affordable, quality small restaurant in the heart of downtown San Jose.
